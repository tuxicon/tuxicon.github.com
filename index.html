I'm currently a grad student at Princeton University. I graduated from IIIT-Delhi in 2013 with a bachelor's degree in computer science. I like to build software that makes people happy. I also particularly enjoy working on research projects that help such software become crazy smart, super fast and dirt cheap.

Send me an email, friend me on facebook or follow me on twitter. You can also check out my blog. For a short summary of my work, my resume might also be helpful.

My broad research area is operating systems and networks. I am particularly interested in building and deploying large-scale networked software systems that underly many of the high-performance applications we use today (EC2, Azure, Parse etc.).

I recently built an event aggregator and personalization engine called What's Next Up and a delay-tolerant filesharing network called Bounce.
